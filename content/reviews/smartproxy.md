Smartproxy is a popular choice for individuals and small to medium-sized businesses who need a reliable and affordable proxy service. They offer a user-friendly dashboard and a wide range of proxy types.

## Key Features

- **Proxy Network:** Over 40 million IPs in 195+ countries.
- **Proxy Types:** Residential, datacenter, and dedicated datacenter proxies.
- **X-Browser:** A multi-session browser for managing multiple accounts.
- **Chrome and Firefox Extensions:** Easy-to-use browser extensions for managing proxies.

## Pricing

Smartproxy's pricing is one of its main selling points. They offer a variety of plans that are more affordable than many of the top-tier providers.

- **Residential Proxies:** Starting from $12.5/GB.
- **Datacenter Proxies:** Starting from $0.7/GB.

## Performance

We tested Smartproxy's residential proxies on a variety of targets and found them to be reliable and effective. While their success rates were slightly lower than Bright Data and Oxylabs, they were still well above 99%.

## Conclusion

Smartproxy is an excellent choice for users who need a reliable and affordable proxy service. Their user-friendly interface and competitive pricing make them a great option for small to medium-sized businesses, as well as individuals who are just getting started with web scraping.
