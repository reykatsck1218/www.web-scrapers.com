Not every project needs to build a scraper. Bright Data's Datasets give you easily accessible, structured, and accurate public web data for any use case — either from the marketplace or built custom to your exact requirements.

## About

Datasets can be filtered into subsets for a cost-effective, time-saving solution. When a dataset isn't already available in Bright Data's marketplace, a custom dataset can be built to your specifications. Data is available in various formats and delivered via email, API, Webhook, Google Cloud, Amazon S3, or Azure.

## Best For

- Buyers who want immediate purchase and delivery from the marketplace
- Businesses with specific requirements that need a customized dataset

## Key Benefits

- Data coverage, accuracy, and structure ensured
- Datasets maintained as website structures change
- Cost-effective, time-saving **subsets** with laser-focused data
- Data extraction **100% compliant** with all data protection laws (GDPR & CCPA)
- Custom output fields to meet specific business requirements
- **Data feed** of new/updated records on a predefined schedule
