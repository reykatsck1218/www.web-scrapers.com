Choosing the right web scraping platform is crucial for success. Bright Data and Oxylabs are two of the biggest names in the industry, but they have different strengths and weaknesses. In this comparison, we'll help you decide which one is the best fit for your needs.

## Proxy Network

- **Bright Data:** Over 72 million IPs.
- **Oxylabs:** Over 100 million IPs.

Oxylabs has a larger proxy network, but both platforms offer a wide range of locations and IP types.

## Features

- **Bright Data:** Focus on proxy services and a powerful Web Unlocker.
- **Oxylabs:** Focus on AI-powered scraper APIs for different use cases.

Bright Data is a great choice if you need a lot of control over your proxy setup. Oxylabs is a better choice if you want to offload the scraping process to an API.

## Pricing

- **Bright Data:** Generally more expensive, but offers a pay-as-you-go plan.
- **Oxylabs:** Competitive pricing, with a focus on monthly and yearly plans.

The best choice for you will depend on your budget and usage.

## Conclusion

- **Use Bright Data if:** You need a large, powerful proxy network and want a lot of control over your setup.
- **Use Oxylabs if:** You want to use AI-powered scraper APIs to simplify the data collection process.
